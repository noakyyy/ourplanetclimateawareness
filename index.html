About this site

This site is an educational resource that gives concise, actionable information about climate change. It is intentionally lightweight and accessible, built with semantic HTML, minimal JavaScript, and responsive CSS so it performs on low-bandwidth networks and mobile devices.

Primary causes

Fossil fuels

Energy production and transport burn coal, oil, and gas, releasing CO₂ into the atmosphere.

Land use

Deforestation and land conversion reduce carbon sinks and increase emissions from soils and biomass.

Agriculture

Livestock and rice paddies produce methane; fertilizers release nitrous oxide — potent greenhouse gases.

Observable effects

  • More frequent and severe heatwaves and wildfires
  • Stronger storms, coastal flooding, and sea-level rise
  • Shifts in rainfall patterns, harming agriculture
  • Ocean warming and acidification, stressing marine ecosystems
  • Impacts disproportionately hit vulnerable communities

What you can do — practical actions

At home

  • Improve insulation and reduce wasted heating/cooling
  • Switch to LED lighting and energy-efficient appliances
  • Choose renewable electricity plans where available

Travel

  • Use public transport, cycle, or walk when possible
  • Fly less; prefer direct flights if necessary
  • Consider electric or hybrid vehicles

Community & policy

  • Vote for climate-aware policies and leaders
  • Support local conservation and adaptation projects
  • Encourage businesses to disclose emissions and set targets
